About me

I am an Ellis PhD student at Polytechnic of Turin, Italy. My research interests include Deep Learning, Computer Vision, Edge AI and Egocentric Vision. I’m particularly interested in online applications of Computer Vision models such as Online Action Detection or Online Action Anticipation. My supervisor is Professor Barbara Caputo and I am a member of the Visual And Multimodal Applied Learning Laboratory @ Politecnico di Torino.

Previously, I completed both my MSc in Data Science and Engineering and my BSc in Computer Engineering at Polytechnic of Turin.
